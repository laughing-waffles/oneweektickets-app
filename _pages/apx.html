Problem

How do I determine what I should charge for a live event? Traditional sources of data for determining ticket prices leads to significant revenue leakage.

65%+

Concert tickets unsold

20%

Tickets resold to the secondary market

$15B

revenue lost to the secondary market

Solution

Ask people what they are willing to pay. OneWeek Tickets conducts price discovery through a first-price sealed-bid auction. Ticket buyers bid what they are willing to pay and don’t know what others bid.
