Problem

How do artists determine what they should charge for a live event? Traditional sources of data for determining ticket prices leads to significant revenue leakage.

Our Solution

Ask people what they are willing to pay. OneWeek Tickets conducts price discovery through a first-price sealed-bid auction. Ticket buyers bid what they are willing to pay and donâ€™t know what others bid.
