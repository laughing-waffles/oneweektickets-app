What is
One Week?

One Week is a ticket sales platform that helps venues, artists, and promoters optimize ticket pricing.

We use a unique auction design that is fan-friendly and reduces secondary market incentives.

Sealed Auctions

Our unique 7 auctions over 7 days mechanism allows for a mutually-beneficial process to determine what price the market is willing to bear.

Price Discovery

Discovering the market-equilibrium price for your tickets means that any tickets not sold via the auction can be sold at the optimized price.

Artist Support

Auctioning tickets ensures that fans can support artists at a price fans believe is fair, instead of paying inflated secondary market prices.

How does One Week work?

01Create

Determine how many tickets you want to allocate to One Week. Our data shows, depending on maxium capacity, that at least 10% to 25% of tickets should be allocated in order to gain enough insight for price discovery. We can handle as many or as few tickets as you see fit.

02Auction

The One Week auction releases only 1/7 of the total ticket quantity each day, every day for seven days. If a fan bids and isn't in the top 14% of bids that day, their bid remains active in future auction days.

03Nudge

One Week provides unsuccessful bidders with feedback on how to succeed in the next day's auction. Fans can maintain or modify their bid in future auctions with one-click from their email.

04Maximize

After seven rounds of bidding, One Week has discovered the market equilibrium price. This price is the optimal face value price tickets could be sold at after the auction concludes to maximize sales volumes and revenues.
