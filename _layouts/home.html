About
OneWeek

OneWeek is a ticketing platform that helps venues, artists, promoters, and fans all have a better experience.

We use a unique auction design that optimizes ticket pricing for buyers and sellers while also reducing secondary market incentives.

Price Discovery

Knowing how to price your product is complex in any industry. The traditional methods of pricing event tickets do not allow for price discovery because of market and product desirability fluctuations between events. We built OneWeek to solve this problem on behalf of venues, artists, and promoters.

Revenue

OneWeek's unique 7-day rolling auction design allows for the optimal ticket price to be discovered by engaging buyers and sellers in a mutually-beneficial process to determine what price the market is willing to bear. When the auction concludes, OneWeek continues to sell unsold tickets at the optimized price.

Volume

Discovering the market-equilibrium price for your tickets means that any tickets left unsold at the end of the auction period can be sold at the optimized price that will give you the best chance at selling all of your tickets without leaving money on the table from under-pricing your tickets.

Scalping

Scalpers and the secondary market lose their incentive to scoop up your tickets because real fans will outbid them. Any margin they might have hoped to garner by reselling your tickets at a higher price will be marginalized by the market-equilibrium price discovered at the end of the auction period.

Accessible

Allowing fans to determine your tickets' fair market price means that they gain access to your event at a price they can bear. This prevents fans from being victimized in the secondary market and ensures that their money supports the primary market directly.

Artist Support

Fans want to support artists directly and are endlessly frustrated when forced to engage the secondary market to access events. Selling tickets through a primary-market auction ensures that fans can support artists directly at a fair price.

How does OneWeek work?

01Create

Create an event with the OneWeek admin panel just as you would with any other ticketing platform. Define your ticket categories and set your secret minimum price to protect against significant losses from low bids.

02Auction

The OneWeek auction design is unique as it releases only 1/7 of the total ticket quantity each day, every day for a week. This allows for patrons and sellers alike to respond to the unique market demands for your event.

03Nudge

OneWeek provides unsuccessful bidders with feedback regarding how much they should raise their bid to succeed the next day. Additionally, sellers receive daily reports helping them determine if they need to reduce their secret minimum price.

04Maximize

After seven rounds of bidding, OneWeek will discover the market equilibrium price. This price is the optimal face value price tickets should be sold at after the auction concludes to maximize sales volumes and revenues.
